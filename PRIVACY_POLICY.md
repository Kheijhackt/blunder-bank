# Privacy Policy for Blunder Bank

**Last Updated:** March 21, 2026

## 1. Hi There!

Welcome to **Blunder Bank**. This is a **personal hobby project** created and maintained solely by **me**. There is no company behind this app—just one developer trying to build a cool chess tool.

This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and protect your information. By using Blunder Bank, you agree to these simple practices.

> **⚠️ Quick Disclaimer:** This app is provided **"as is"** for learning and fun. I don't guarantee it will be bug-free or always online. Since I'm just one person working on this in my spare time, resources are limited!

## 2. What Information I Collect

I only collect what's absolutely necessary to make the app work.

### A. Things You Give Me

- **Account Details:** When you sign up, I store your **name**, **email address**, and a **hashed password**.
    - _Note:_ Your name and email **don't have to be real**! You can use a nickname. I just need a valid email so you can reset your password if you forget it, although this isn't an implemented feature as of now.
- **Chess Data:** The blunders you manually add, including:
    - Chess positions (FEN text)
    - The correct move
    - Your personal notes and analysis
    - Your ELO rating at the time
    - Opening names and links to source games
- **Feedback:** If you click the "Send Feedback" button, you'll go to a **Google Form**. Anything you type there is handled by Google, not stored in my database.

### B. Things Collected Automatically

- **Stats:** To help you improve, I save your accuracy %, practice streaks, and when you last practiced.
- **Preferences:** I remember if you like **Dark Mode** or **Light Mode**.
- **Server Logs:** My host (**Laravel Cloud**) automatically records basic technical stuff (like IP addresses and request times) to keep the server running safely. Since I'm on the free tier, **I don't look at, analyze, or sell these logs.** They just exist for security and error fixing.

## 3. How I Use Your Info

I use your data for exactly three reasons:

1.  To let you log in and manage your account.
2.  To show you your chess blunders and run the spaced repetition algorithm.
3.  To remember your settings (like Dark Mode) and keep the site secure.

**I do NOT sell, rent, or trade your data to anyone. Ever.**

## 4. Where Your Data Lives

- **Hosting:** The app runs on **Laravel Cloud**.
- **Location:** Your database is located in **Mumbai, South Asia**.
- **How Long?** I keep your data **indefinitely** until you delete it. I don't automatically wipe inactive accounts right now, so your blunders will be there whenever you come back.

## **5. Third-Party Services**

I keep things simple and don't use many outside services:

- **Laravel Cloud:** They host the website. Like all hosting providers, they automatically handle basic technical data (like server uptime and traffic routing) to keep the site running. **They don't sell your data**, and I don't give them access to your chess blunders or account details.
- **Google Forms:** Used only for feedback. Google handles that data (see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)).
- **No Analytics:** I **don't** use Google Analytics, ads, or tracking scripts.

## 6. Security

As a solo developer, I do my best to keep things safe:

- Passwords are hashed (scrambled) before saving.
- The site uses HTTPS encryption.
- Laravel handles session security automatically.

But remember: **No website is 100% hack-proof.** Since this is a hobby project without a dedicated security team, please use it at your own risk.

## 7. Your Rights

It's your data, so you're in control:

- **View:** See all your data in your dashboard.
- **Edit:** Change any blunder or note anytime.
- **Delete:** Remove individual cards or your whole account via the settings page. (If you can't find the button, just send me feedback!)

## 8. Age Stuff

Blunder Bank is for chess players of all ages. I don't check IDs or verify ages. If you're under 13 (or the legal age in your country), please ask a parent before signing up.

## 9. Changes

Since I'm still building and tweaking this project, I might update this policy occasionally. I'll try to let you know if something big changes, but feel free to check back here now and then.
